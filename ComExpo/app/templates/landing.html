FAQs

Common questions about our classroom booking platform.

How do I book a room?

Log in to your account, select the desired classroom, choose a time slot, and confirm your booking. It's that simple.

Are bookings free?

Most classroom bookings are free for registered students. Some specialized spaces may have additional requirements.

How far in advance can I book?

Students can book rooms up to two weeks in advance. Priority is given to academic and group study purposes.

What if no rooms are available?

Check alternative time slots or nearby classrooms. Our system provides real-time availability updates.
