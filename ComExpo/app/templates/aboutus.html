About RoomRak

Smart, simple, and efficient classroom booking
for King Mongkut's University students.

Our Story

RoomRak is an initiative by students of King Mongkut’s University, designed to simplify and modernize classroom booking. We noticed how much time students spent trying to find free study spaces — and we wanted to fix that.

Our mission is to make the process of reserving classrooms fast, transparent, and accessible to everyone. Whether you’re preparing for exams, working on group projects, or simply need a quiet spot to focus, RoomRak makes it easy to book the right room in just a few clicks.

“Empowering students to learn, collaborate, and grow — one room at a time.”
