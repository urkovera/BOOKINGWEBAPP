About RoomRak

Smart, simple, and efficient classroom booking for
The King Mongkut's University of Technology Thonburi students.

Our Story

RoomRak is an initiative by students of King Mongkut’s University of Technology Thonburi. We noticed how much time students spent walking around trying to find free study spaces — and we wanted to fix that.

Whether you’re preparing for exams, working on group projects, or simply need a quiet spot to focus, RoomRak makes it easy to book the right room in just a few clicks.

Our Mission

To make the process of reserving classrooms fast, transparent, and accessible to everyone, reducing administrative friction for both students and professors.

Our Vision

A campus where space is utilized efficiently, empowering students to learn, collaborate, and grow without logistical barriers.

"Empowering students to learn, collaborate, and grow — one room at a time."
